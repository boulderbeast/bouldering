Bouldering

Bouldering is a climbing sport that can be practiced both indoor and outdoors. In contrast to lead climbing where you climb up tall walls, bouldering is characterized by short, explosive routes.
I first started bouldering a bit more than a year ago. At first I thought it was terrifying, but now I love it!

What's so special about bouldering?

  • You climb small rock formations or artificial rock walls
  • Routes are max. 4.5 meters high
  • No ropes are used (but if you fall, a mat will catch you)

Three reasons to start bouldering

  1. You can climb with friends, regardless of their skill or fitness level
  2. There are routes for each level, so you can always find something that isn't too hard but still challenges you
  3. People in bouldering gyms are generally very willing to help you if you're stuck!
